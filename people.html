Meet the Research Team

You'll find a short description of the undergraduate and postgraduate students working in Civil Systems Engineering in the Peer Research Lab below. We're just getting started... Interested in joining us? Contact me.

Postgraduate Students

Karan Titus

Karan is a first year PhD student studying the feasibility of using biomass carbon capture and storage (BECCS) in combination with geothermal power generation for carbon-negative geothermal energy. Karan is co-advised with Dr. David Dempsey (University of Canterbury).

Sam Archie

Sam is a Masters student studying multi-criteria optimisation of urban spatial plans. His research aims to help better plan for sustainable and reslient development and growth in cities. Sam's supervisory team is led by Dr. Tom Logan (University of Canterbury).
